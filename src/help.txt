Welcome! This is Help Window for Enigma Cipher Machine!

Enigma greets you with the messages and the following menu:
*Enigma Options*"
"1. Encrypt the message."
"2. Decrypt the message."
"3. Look at the Enigma Historical Information."
"4. Study attack methods."
"5. Help."
"6. Exit."

At the beginning of work you can choose any number to
start working with different aspects of Enigma.

1. Encryption. 
* You will recieve a message asking you to configure rotors.
It means, you have to enter different numbers 1-5 symbolizing the unique "names" of the rotors.
If you are mistaken or misspelled, you will ll recieve an error message and will have to start 
configuring rotors again.
* After setting up rotors, you will have to configure rings. Enter 3 alphabetic chars from 'A' to 'Z'. 
Upper or lowercase accepted. In case of an input mistake, the warning will be thrown.
* Afterwards, you will have an opportunity to set up a plugboard or (in german) Steckerbrett. It is optional.
If you desired to configure it, enter no more than 13 pairs of different latin letters, i.e. ML, RT, etc.
* After finishing settings of Enigma, enter the secret ,essage you want to encrypt. 
It is possible to write a long text with spaces.
* To finish working with Enigma press 'Enter'.

2. Decryption.

3. Enigma Historical Information.
You will recieve a file with interesting information and histoeical facts about Enigma.

4. Study attack methods.

5. Help.
You will see the current manual.

6. Exit.
You will stop the Enigma Cipher Machine.
